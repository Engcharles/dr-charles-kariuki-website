About Dr. Charles Kariuki

Dr. Charles Kariuki is a renowned life coach, motivational speaker, and author with over two decades of experience. Having authored over 100 books on personal development, masculinity, relationships, and more, he inspires countless individuals to unlock their potential. His titles, such as *Digital Kid Dominator* and *The Lion Mindset*, offer practical tools for transformation. A passionate advocate for youth and men’s issues, Dr. Kariuki’s work as a trainer and team-building expert has empowered organizations worldwide.
